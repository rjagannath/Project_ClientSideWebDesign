About Us

Hospitality Management Chicago is a website developed to render customers with multiple options for booking rooms, restaurants and hang-out places in and around the beautiful city of Chicago.

This design and development of the website will be credited to four individuals namely Rohini, Parul, Komal and Rohit under the guidance of Prof. Nick Hayward.

What we do

We provide both direct and online services to customers willing to book rooms, restaurants and hangout places. The availability of the resources is subject to season(duration of the year) and priority bookings.
